ABOUT THIS SITE

Here to help you in aything related to gaming!

I created this site to let you gamers where to look when you're stuck. Whether it's getting stuck on a level, needing some advice on gameplay or to become a pro in gaming, you can count on me to help you anytime! Back when I was young, there were times when I badly needed someone to tell me how to get past this boss, develop strategies for finishing challenges, stuff like that. Unfortunately, there were not much people to help me and I felt tired, fail after fail, on figthing bosses in Dark Souls(oh boy, it sure is challenging!). Therefore, I decided to help those who are in the same state that I was before, so that they don't lose interest in the game. Game on, gamers!
